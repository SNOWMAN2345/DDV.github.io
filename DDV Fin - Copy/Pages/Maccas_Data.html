Factors behind student consumption of Fast-Food

The most popular time for McDonalds is at the end of the year, then as the new year starts, sales drop rapidly, before climbing back up at the end of the year

A potential reason for the rapid drop is New Year Resolutions, and people wanting to diet away from McDonalds.

9% of students say they have a family tradition which involves the consumption of fast food.

Common traditions include: Eating out on fridays, Birthdays and Special Celebrations

Having a time constraint is the main reason students are consuming fast food.
